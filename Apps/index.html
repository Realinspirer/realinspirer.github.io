Due Tracker

Tracking client dues and details can be so much pain, especially when you are a small/medium business, and have to track hundreds and thousands of client dues entries every week. This app is a simple and effective way to track all those data easily. Just enter the data and forget about managing it!

Some features:

  • Clean and modern UI to make working with the app simple and hassle-free.
  • Cross-platform application. Windows & Android supported. Keep your data wherever you go!
  • You can add client and data effortlessly! The entered data can be edited and deleted as required!
  • The app contains many small lifestyle improving features to quickly add data, and also dates!
  • This app consists of ways to add clients in bulk, where you can enter all the required data first, and then add and review all of them altogether!
  • With just one click, you can get a record and net due for any of your saved client! Save hours and hours of time finding a client's outstanding dues when they ask for it.
  • You can also add images of your client along with other details in the comments. You can also request me for additional detail types.
  • This app can also track date and time of entered data. This app has powerful tools to assign date and time to really simplify your work!
  • The app has support for exporting and recovering data. The same feature can also be used to update the data across devices (manually)! On Windows, this app can directly backup onto your OneDrive folder, where it can be picked up by OneDrive and saved in the cloud!
  • This app can generate an invoice that you can provide to the client in a click of a button! The range of the exported data as well as the whole invoice is customizable, and you can export as an image file or .HTML.

...and much- much more!

Items Tracker

Keep information of all the products and their prices using this app! This app includes several ways to make keeping track of products easier and efficient.

Some features:

  • Clean and modern UI to make working with the app simple and hassle-free.
  • Cross-platform application. Windows & Android supported. Keep your data wherever you go!
  • Easily add, edit, delete entries as required. You can add three integer values labeled as "Price", "Discounted Price", and "Bought Price". String values include title, and comments. You can request me to add or remove or edit labels as required.
  • The app also keeps record of last edited time and date.
  • Advanced backup and recovery features. This app can also backup it's files directly onto your OneDrive folder on Windows, from where OneDrive can pick it up and store it in the cloud. You can use this feature to keep applications on different devices in sync.
  • You can generate QR code for the entries, which can be scanned in the app itself to show you the required data! You can print the QR codes and stick them to places, which can make keeping track of those items even easier!
  • You can also generate QR code on the go without adding them first!

ImageSHOT!

Arrange set of photos in a grid. ImageSHOT! is a powerful collage-like image creator where you can put photos in a grid effortlessly. You can use it to export passport-sized photos, multiple images in the same page for printing, and so on.

Some features:

  • Clean and modern UI to make working with the app simple and hassle-free.
  • Cross-platform application. Windows & Android supported. Export images on the go!
  • This app offers tons of customizations, and tweaks you can do. Edit page sizes, grid cell sizes, and also resolution multipliers for the purpose of exporting and printing!
  • This app also includes profile support. You can create a profile with all the required dimensions, and then save the profile with a custom name! You can also delete the profile as needed, or set a particular profile as default!
  • On Windows, this app can directly read images from your clipboard!
  • The app provides you with user-friendly clickable grid cells which you can click with a selected image to assign the grid cell it's image.
  • You can also add text labels to each grid for more efficient work! You can also customize the label's font size!
  • You can also lock grid cells that prevents you from accidentally removing or editing a previously assigned grid cell.

...and much- much more!
